A comprehensive Power BI interactive dashboard designed to analyze retail sales performance across different regions, timeframes, and product categories. This project transforms raw "SuperMarket" transaction data into actionable business insights.

🚀 Key Features & Visualizations

KPI Cards: Real-time tracking of Total Sales (2.26M) and Total Order Volume (9.8K).
Sales Trends: A line chart illustrating growth trajectory from 2015 to 2018, showing a significant upward trend.
Regional Analysis: Funnel chart comparing order counts across West, East, Central, and South regions.
Shipping Insights: A pie chart breaking down delivery preferences (Standard Class accounts for nearly 60% of shipments).
Product Deep-Dive: Horizontal bar charts for Sub-Category performance (Binders and Paper leading) and vertical charts for Category and Segment sales.
Dynamic Filtering: Sidebar slicers for Month and top-level slicers for Region allow for granular data exploration.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Tool: Power BI Desktop

Data Source: Superstore/Supermarket Dataset (CSV/Excel)

Data Modeling: Star Schema / Flat Table (as seen in the "train" data pane)

Design: Custom Dark Theme with high-contrast neon accents for readability.

💡 Business Insights Derived
Shipping Efficiency: The heavy reliance on "Standard Class" suggests a cost-sensitive customer base, or an opportunity to promote premium shipping tiers.

Top Performers: The "Office Supplies" category significantly outperforms "Furniture" and "Technology" in terms of order frequency.

Growth: Sales have seen a consistent year-over-year increase, peaking in 2018.
